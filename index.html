Caring for your pig

Guiney pigs are a lot of work. (we have two) Every day, we scoop poop out of the cage

and feed them. We also have an auto feeder for them. The standard diet for them is timothy

hay or pellets, and most vegetables and fruits. For bedding we use wood chips from Tractor Supply.

They like wood scraps to chew on, and huches to hide in.
